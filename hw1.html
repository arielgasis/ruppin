About Oranges

Oranges are citrus fruits known for their vibrant color, juicy flesh, and tangy taste. They are rich in vitamin C and are often consumed fresh, juiced, or used in various culinary recipes.

Health Benefits

Oranges are rich sources of vitamin C and antioxidants. They support the immune system, promote healthy skin, and aid in digestion.
